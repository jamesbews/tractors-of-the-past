Project: 1944 Case D

I purchased my first tractor this November. The tractor is a 1944 Case D, the same tractor my grandfather used growing up in the 40's. As far as I know, the tractor has been in working condition its whole life. My plan for the tractor is to completely restore the tractor to its orginal condition.
